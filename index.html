List of Abilities

Routing

These abilities are about unlocking new routes: pathways and passages to new areas and shortcuts to old areas.

Range (Jumps)

Increased Jump Height

Increases the height of the primary jump. May also increase the height of all other types of jumps (eg. Wall Jump)

    Secondary Jumps

    Allows the protagonist to perform a second jump in mid-air. May allow more jumps as part of a single power-up or multiple powerups. May be a collectible, temporary powerup which allows to regenerate the second jump in mid-air (eg. Jumper).

      Infinite Jump

      Variation of Secondary Jumps

        Wall Jump

        Allows jumping off the wall in mid-air. May disallow climbing a single column by pushing the protagonist far away from the wall on jump that they get back to the wall a little lower than where they jumped from.

        • Super Metroid: Standard Wall Jump which allows vertical climbing, available from the very beginning of the game.
